I need my content translated on a regular basis. Can I get a special rate?

Yes. We love to support content producers. If you have a regular need for translation (daily, weekly, monthly), reach out to us and we can work out a favorable rate that fits better with your budget.

I have an urgent request. How fast can you deliver it?

We can help you meet your deadlines fast. With express delivery, we turbocharge our translation to 1,000 words per hour for just 1 additional cent per word. So for example, if a project is for 45,000 words, we can have it translated with express delivery in under 48 hours for $0.17/word.

I run a non-profit. Can I get a special rate?

Yes. We value working with non-profits who have a regular need for translation services. Reach out to us and we'll be happy to help you keep your non-profit on budget.
